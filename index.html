Commander Shepard

Commander Shepard, Butcher of Torfan, Destroyer of Feros, Bane of Shiala, Betrayer of Gianna, Purger of Rachni, Usurper of the Council, Dog of Cerberus, Krogan-blight, Quarian-curse, and Scourge of the Geth. A war criminal of countless horrendous crimes hunted by every security agency in the universe. He creates destruction everwhere he appears. Avoid at all costs.

Commander Shepard is the protagonist of the popular Bioware game series Mass Effect
